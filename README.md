# 🤖 AI Impact on Jobs & Salaries Dashboard – Power BI 

---

## 📝 1. Project Title  
**Analysis and Visualization of AI Impact on Jobs, Salaries, and Automation Risk Using AWS & PowerBI**

---

## 🎯 2. Short Description 
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of how Artificial Intelligence is impacting **jobs, salaries, and automation risk** across industries.  
The dashboard is built using **Power BI** to transform raw data into **interactive and actionable insights**, helping understand whether AI is replacing jobs or transforming them.

---

## 🧰 3. Tech Stack  
- **AWS S3** – Data storage  
- **AWS Glue (ETL Jobs)** – Data cleaning and transformation  
- **Amazon Athena** – SQL-based data querying  
- **ODBC Connector** – Connecting Athena to Power BI  
- **Power BI** – Dashboard creation, visualization & interactivity  
- **DAX** – Measures for KPIs, salary calculations, and percentages  
- **Data Modeling** – Structured model for efficient reporting  

---

## 📥 4. Data Source  
- **Dataset sourced from Kaggle**  
- Dataset fields include:  
  - Job Role  
  - Industry  
  - Salary Before AI  
  - Salary After AI  
  - Job Status (Unaffected / Modified / Replaced)  
  - Automation Risk Level  
  - AI Adoption Impact  

---

## ⭐ 5. Features  

### 🔹 Interactive KPI Dashboard  
- Average Salary Before AI  
- Average Salary After AI  
- Percentage of Jobs Replaced  
- KPI-based summary using DAX measures  

---

### 🔹 Job Impact Analysis  
- Distribution of job status (Unaffected, Modified, Replaced)  
- Visualized using donut chart  
- Helps understand overall workforce transformation  

---

### 🔹 Salary Analysis Across Industries  
- Comparison of salary before and after AI adoption  
- Industry-wise salary trends using bar charts  
- Identifies sectors with highest growth  

---

### 🔹 Automation Risk Analysis  
- Industry-wise automation risk levels  
- Helps identify high-risk job sectors  

---

### 🔹 Interactive Filters (Slicers)  
- Industry  
- Job Role / Category  

Allows users to explore insights dynamically across different segments.

---

## 🧩 6. Problem Solved by the Dashboard  

With the rise of AI, organizations face uncertainty about its impact on jobs and salaries. Raw data alone does not provide clear answers.  

This dashboard helps to:  
- Understand whether AI is replacing or transforming jobs  
- Analyze salary changes across industries  
- Identify high automation risk sectors  
- Support data-driven workforce and business decisions  

It converts complex data into **clear, visual, and actionable insights**.

---

## ❓ 7. Key Questions Answered  

- How many jobs are replaced vs modified vs unaffected?  
- Are salaries increasing after AI adoption?  
- Which industries are most impacted by AI?  
- Which sectors face the highest automation risk?  
- How does AI impact vary across job roles?
